SEBI and Stock Exchanges Revise Surveillance Framework for Small-Cap Firms
SEBI ଏବଂ ଷ୍ଟକ୍ ଏକ୍ସଚେଞ୍ଜ୍ ଛୋଟ କ୍ୟାପ୍ କମ୍ପାନୀ ନିମିତ୍ତେ ନୂତନ ନିରୀକ୍ଷଣ ନୀତି ଘୋଷଣା କଲେ
The Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) along with stock exchanges introduced a revised surveillance framework to enhance oversight and protection for small-cap companies. The changes aim to prevent manipulation and safeguard investor interests.

RBI Caps Investment by Banks, NBFCs at 20% of AIF Corpus
RBI ବ୍ୟାଙ୍କ ଏବଂ NBFC ଦ୍ୱାରା AIFରେ 20% ରୁ ଅଧିକ ବିନିଯୋଗ ଉପରେ ବାମ୍ଧବାଧ କଲା
The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) mandated that banks and Non-Banking Financial Companies (NBFCs) cannot invest more than 20% of Alternative Investment Funds (AIF) corpus.
ଆର୍ବିଆଇ ବ୍ୟାଙ୍କ ଏବଂ ବ୍ୟାଙ୍କ ବିତିତ ବିତ୍ତୀୟ ସଂସ୍ଥାମାନଙ୍କୁ AIFର କୋଷ ମୁଦ୍ରାର 20% ରୁ ଅଧିକ ବିନିଯୋଗ କରିବାରେ ନିଷେଧ ଆଣିଛି |
